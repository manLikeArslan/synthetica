****5\. How do medical billing services work?****

The medical billing process typically involves the following:  
1\. **Patient Visit:** The patient receives care.  
2\. **Verification:** Insurance details are verified.  
3\. **Coding:** Appropriate diagnosis and procedure codes are assigned.  
4\. **Claim Submission:** Claims are submitted to insurance companies for reimbursement.  
5\. **Insurance Review:** Claims are reviewed for accuracy and policy compliance.  
6\. **Claim Decision:** Claims are approved or denied.  
7\. **Reimbursement:** Approved claims are reimbursed; denied claims are corrected and resubmitted.
