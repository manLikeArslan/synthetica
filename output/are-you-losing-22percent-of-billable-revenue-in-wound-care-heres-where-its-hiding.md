The Silent Impact of Billing Errors
-----------------------------------

Many billing partners treat wound care like any other specialty. They use generalized templates, overlook nuanced procedure combinations, and fail to keep up with payer rule changes.

Here’s what often gets missed:

*   **Undercoded procedures**: Advanced treatments like skin grafting and negative pressure wound therapy (NPWT) require accurate [CPT code](https://www.aapc.com/resources/what-is-cpt) application. A small mistake here can mean thousands in missed reimbursements each month.
*   **Improper modifier use**: Modifiers that signal multiple treatments, frequency exceptions, or distinct procedural services are essential to avoid denials.
*   **Documentation breakdowns**: Even when care is delivered properly, incomplete documentation can lead to rejections or downcoding.
