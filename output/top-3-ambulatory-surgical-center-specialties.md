### **1\. What is an Ambulatory Surgical Center (ASC)?**

An ASC is a healthcare facility where surgeries are performed without requiring an overnight hospital stay. These centers offer cost-effective, outpatient procedures such as plastic surgery, gastrointestinal procedures, and ENT surgeries.

### **2\. Why are outpatient procedures becoming more popular?**

Outpatient procedures are gaining popularity due to their lower costs, advanced safety measures, and shorter recovery times. They eliminate the need for inpatient admission, making them convenient for both patients and healthcare providers.

### **3\. What are the top specialties performed in ASCs?**

Some of the leading specialties include plastic surgery (e.g., tummy tucks, breast implants), gastrointestinal procedures (e.g., colonoscopies, hemorrhoidectomies), and ENT surgeries (e.g., tonsillectomies, laser vision correction).
