*Original URL: https://docsmedicalbilling.com/complete-guide-to-dental-billing-and-cdt-codes/*

While dental billing shares some similarities with traditional medical billing, there are critical differences that make it a specialized field requiring dedicated expertise. Understanding these distinctions is essential for maximizing reimbursements and minimizing claim denials.

Successful dental billing requires a systematic approach from patient check-in through payment posting. Docs Medical Billing, LLC has developed a proven workflow that minimizes denials and accelerates payment cycles.

The CDT code set divides dental procedures into categories by service type. Familiarity with these categories is essential for accurate dental billing and coding.
