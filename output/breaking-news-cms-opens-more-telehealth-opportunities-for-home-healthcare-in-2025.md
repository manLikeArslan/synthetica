Key Telehealth Updates for Home Healthcare in 2025
--------------------------------------------------

### 1\. **Expanded Coverage for Remote Patient Monitoring (RPM)**

*   CMS is increasing reimbursement for RPM services, allowing home health providers to monitor chronic conditions more effectively.
*   **Home Healthcare in 2025** will benefit from improved remote monitoring options for patients with chronic diseases.

### 2\. **Increased Reimbursement for Virtual Home Health Visits**

*   Virtual visits will now be reimbursed at higher rates, reducing the financial burden on home health providers.
*   These changes encourage the integration of telehealth into daily care plans, benefiting both patients and providers offering **Home Healthcare**.

### 3\. **Telehealth for Behavioral and Mental Health Services**

*   CMS is expanding telehealth access for behavioral and mental health services in **Home Healthcare**.
*   Providers can now offer virtual therapy sessions, improving access to essential mental health care.

### 4\. **Easier Compliance with HIPAA and Telehealth Guidelines**

*   CMS has relaxed some regulations to make it easier for providers to comply with telehealth documentation and privacy requirements.
*   This ensures seamless integration of telehealth into **Home Healthcare** without compromising patient data security.
