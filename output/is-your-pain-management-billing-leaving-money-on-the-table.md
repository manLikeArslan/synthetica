### **3 Must-Track RCM Metrics for Better Billing Results:**

An efficient billing process hinges on tracking key performance indicators (KPIs). For pain management billing, some crucial RCM KPIs include:

1.  **Days in Accounts Receivable (A/R)**: This measures how long it takes to collect payments. The **formula: (Total A/R ÷ Average Daily Charges)**. Keeping this number below 40 days is ideal.
2.  **Denial Rate**: A high denial rate directly impacts your cash flow. **Formula: (Number of Denied Claims ÷ Total Claims Submitted)**.
3.  **First Pass Resolution Rate (FPRR)**: This KPI measures the percentage of claims paid on the first submission. **Formula: (Clean Claims Paid on First Submission ÷ Total Claims Submitted)**.

These KPIs help pain management practices maintain financial stability by highlighting areas of improvement in the billing cycle.
