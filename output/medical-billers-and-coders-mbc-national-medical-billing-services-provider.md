Understanding Medical Billing and Coding
----------------------------------------

Before delving into the services provided by us, it is essential to understand the fundamental concepts of medical billing and coding. [Medical billing](https://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/medical-billing-services.aspx) involves the process of submitting and following up on claims with health insurance companies to receive payment for services rendered by healthcare providers.

On the other hand, [medical coding](https://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/medical-coding-services.aspx) is the translation of diagnoses, procedures, and services into universally recognized alphanumeric codes, facilitating accurate billing and record-keeping.
