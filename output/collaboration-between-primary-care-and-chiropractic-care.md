**1\. How does chiropractic care integrate with primary care?**

Chiropractic care is increasingly integrated with primary care and other healthcare settings, especially for managing musculoskeletal disorders. This collaboration enhances patient care through a multidisciplinary approach.

**2\. What challenges arise in billing for chiropractic and primary care collaboration?**

The blurred roles and shared responsibilities in collaborative care can complicate billing. Effective communication and clear decision-making are key to managing these complexities and ensuring accurate claims.

**3\. What is the impact of collaboration on patient care?**

Collaboration between chiropractic and primary care physicians can improve patient outcomes, especially in musculoskeletal conditions. However, poor communication can sometimes lead to fragmented care and lower quality.
