How MBC Builds Your Pricing Model?
----------------------------------

We begin with a **consultation to understand your RCM workflow.** Then we analyze key data points:

*   Monthly claim volume
*   Specialty and service lines
*   Reimbursement timelines
*   Current denial rate and AR aging

Based on your needs, we offer three core pricing models:

### **1\.** Percentage of Collections

Ideal for outcome-focused groups that prioritize cash in the door.

### **2\.** Flat-Fee Per Encounter

Great for high-volume specialties like urgent care or family medicine.

### **3\.** Hybrid Model

Mix of percentage and flat-fee to balance costs and complexity.

All pricing includes a **Dedicated Account Manager, full-service billing, denial management, and compliance support.**

FAQs About Flexible Pricing Models for Medical Groups
-----------------------------------------------------

**1\. What are flexible pricing models in medical billing?**

Flexible pricing models are custom billing arrangements based on claim volume, specialty, or collections. They scale with your practice needs.

**2\. How does flexible pricing reduce RCM costs?**

It aligns billing costs with performance and volume. You avoid paying for unused capacity or overpaying for low-volume periods.

**3\. What’s the best pricing model for multi-specialty groups?**

A hybrid model often works best. It combines flat-fee and percentage-based billing for different specialties or services.

**4\. Can MBC support large practices with over 100 providers?**

Yes. MBC is built to support large, multi-site groups with customized pricing, reporting, and account management.
