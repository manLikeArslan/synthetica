**Key CMS Code Updates for Dermatology 2025**
---------------------------------------------

The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) introduces annual revisions to Current Procedural Terminology (CPT) codes, impacting billing processes across specialties. Some key updates for 2025 include:

### **[Dermatology Billing](https://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/speciality/dermatology-medical-billing-services.html):**

*   **CPT 15758** – Flap closure of wounds, emphasizing specific anatomic site coding.
*   **CPT 17312** – Mohs micrographic surgery for complex skin cancers.

### **[Plastic Surgery Billing](https://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/speciality/plastic-surgery-medical-billing-services.html):**

*   **CPT 19380** – Revision of reconstructed breast, ensuring accurate billing for follow-ups.
*   **CPT 15777** – Implantation of biologic implant for reconstructive purposes.

### **Cosmetic Billing:**

*   **CPT 11954** – Injection of dermal fillers, categorized for face and neck.
*   **CPT 15773** – Fat grafting procedures for body contouring.

### **Reconstructive Billing:**

*   **CPT 20926** – Tissue grafting, including autografts and allografts.
*   **CPT 19342** – Immediate breast reconstruction post-mastectomy.

Staying informed and implementing these changes correctly can prevent claim denials and reduce administrative burdens.
