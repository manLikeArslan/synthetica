Challenges in Pulmonology Billing
---------------------------------

Pulmonology billing presents several challenges, including:

*   **Complex CPT and ICD-10 Coding:** Pulmonary function tests, sleep studies, and procedures like bronchoscopy require accurate coding for optimal reimbursement.
*   **Frequent Payer Policy Updates:** Insurance policies for pulmonary services frequently change, making it essential to stay up to date.
*   **High Denial Rates:** Lack of documentation, incorrect modifiers, or coding errors often lead to claim rejections.
*   **Compliance and Regulatory Hurdles:** Meeting HIPAA and Medicare guidelines is critical to avoid audits and penalties.
