**What Are Medical Billing Services?**
--------------------------------------

**Medical billing services** handle the process of submitting, tracking, and managing insurance claims. These services ensure that healthcare providers receive proper reimbursement for their services.

### Here’s what medical billing includes:

*   Verifying patient insurance eligibility
*   [Medical Coding Services](https://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/medical-coding-services.aspx) using ICD-10, CPT, and HCPCS codes
*   Submitting claims to insurance payers
*   Following up on unpaid or denied claims
*   Posting payments and reconciling revenue
*   Sending patient statements and managing collections
