Common Reasons for Revenue Loss in OB/GYN Medical Billing
---------------------------------------------------------

### 1\. Coding Errors

Using incorrect CPT or ICD-10 codes can result in claim rejections and denials.

### 2\. Incomplete Documentation

Missing or incorrect patient information leads to payment delays and claim rejections.

### 3\. Insurance Verification Issues

Not verifying patient insurance before treatment can cause denied claims.

### 4\. Ignoring Denied Claims

If denied claims are not followed up on, practices lose money.

### 5\. Incorrect Modifier Usage

Modifiers are essential in pregnancy billing, and errors in their use can lead to claim rejections.

### 6\. Delayed Credentialing

If providers are not credentialed with insurance payers, claims can be rejected, causing revenue loss.
