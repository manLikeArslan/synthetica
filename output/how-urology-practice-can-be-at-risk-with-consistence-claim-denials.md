FAQs
----

**1\. Why are my insurance claims being denied?**

Claims are often denied due to errors like incorrect CPT codes, incomplete patient information, or non-covered services. These errors can be avoided with accurate data entry and verifying coverage before treatment.

**2\. How can I prevent patient data errors that lead to claim denials?**

Ensure patient identifiers (name, date of birth, insurance number) are accurate by verifying details at every visit. Always request a photocopy of ID documents and insurance cards to avoid discrepancies.

**3\. How can I check if a patient’s insurance coverage is still active?**

Verify insurance benefits and coverage eligibility at least 48 hours before the patient’s visit. Contact the insurance provider to confirm that services are covered and avoid denials.

**4\. What should I do if a procedure requires prior approval or precertification?**

Always confirm with the insurance provider if prior approval is needed before proceeding with the procedure. Inform patients of potential costs upfront to avoid unexpected charges.

**5\. How can I improve my practice’s claim approval rate?**

Focus on front-end registration accuracy, verify insurance eligibility, and collect patient payments upfront. Also, consider hiring certified Urology billers and coders to ensure compliance and reduce errors.
