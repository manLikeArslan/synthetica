**Some Common Denial Codes and Their Solutions**
------------------------------------------------

*   **CO-16:** Claim/Service Lacks Information or Has Submission/Billing Error(s)

This code indicates missing or incorrect information. To resolve this, review the claim thoroughly to ensure all required information is accurate. Cross-check patient charts and verify that all medical codes match.

*   **CO-18:** Duplicate Claim or Service

This occurs when a claim is submitted multiple times for the same service. Review the patient’s billing history and remove any duplicate claims to address this. Ensure the claim is sent to the correct insurance company.

*   **CO-22:** This Care May Be Covered by Another Payer Per Coordination of Benefits

This denial arises when the insurance company believes another provider should cover the claim. Review the patient’s insurance information and submit the claim to the appropriate provider.

*   **CO-29:** The Time Limit for Filing Has Expired

Claims submitted after the filing deadline receive this code. Verify the time limit with the insurance company and request an extension if necessary. Ensure timely resubmission of the claim.
