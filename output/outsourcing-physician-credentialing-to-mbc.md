**FAQs**
--------

**1\. What is provider credentialing?**

Provider credentialing is the process of verifying a physician’s qualifications, licenses, and professional background to ensure they are eligible to participate with insurance payers.

**2\. Why is provider credentialing important for my practice?**

Without properly credentialed providers, your practice risks losing payments from insurance carriers, which can negatively affect revenue and cash flow.

**3\. How can outsourcing credentialing help my practice?**

Outsourcing credentialing reduces the time and cost of managing the process in-house, allowing your staff to focus on patient care while experts handle the complexities of credentialing and enrollment.

**4\. What does the credentialing process include?**

It involves submitting applications to insurance payers, maintaining provider records, updating provider details, handling contract negotiations, and ensuring timely re-certifications for credentials.

**5\. Can outsourcing credentialing reduce costs for my practice?**

Yes, outsourcing credentialing eliminates the need for full-time in-house experts and ensures quicker processing of applications, which can reduce overhead and improve reimbursement rates.
