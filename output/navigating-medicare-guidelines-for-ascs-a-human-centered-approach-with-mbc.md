### **What Procedures Does Medicare Cover?**

Medicare approves outpatient surgeries that are low-risk and don’t require overnight stays. Examples include:

*   Cataract surgeries
*   Colonoscopies
*   Minor orthopedic procedures (e.g., knee arthroscopy)

**Insight:** CMS updates its approved list annually. In 2024, 11 new procedures, including certain spinal interventions, were added. To stay ahead, review the updates every quarter.
