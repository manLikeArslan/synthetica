### 2\. CMS Proposes Two New C-APCs and a 3.1% Increase to OPPS Conversion Factor

The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) is set to create two new C-APCs, dividing the existing Level 2 Intraocular C-APC 5492 into Level 2 and Level 3 Intraocular C-APC 5493. Additionally, a 3.1% increase in the Outpatient Prospective Payment System (OPPS) conversion factor is anticipated for Plastic Surgery.

However, the conversion factor is decreasing by 3.4%, emphasizing the need for practitioners to stay informed and adapt to these changes. This adaptation is where the expertise of medical billing services becomes invaluable.
