Key Terms of Claims Scrubbing Process
-------------------------------------

### Superbill

A superbill is a detailed report of the services patients receive from the providers. It also includes the date and type of services a provider renders.

### Claim Form

Claim scrubbers request payers for the payment by submitting a form/document called a claim form. This form tells why the insurer has to pay a certain amount of medical bills.

### Claims Editing

This component of claims scrubbing in medical billing involves reviewing and replacing incorrect information with authentic information. Claims editing helps analyze and fix errors, leading to limited denials and swift payments.

### Charge Review

Charge review helps compare the practice rate with standard rates determined by Medicaid and Medicare. It ensures compliance and reduces claim denials.

### Coding Errors

Coding errors occur when incorrect information is entered in the form, resulting in claim denials. It impacts cash flow negatively by delaying payment.
