Common Reasons for Claim Denials in Neurology Billing and Credentialing
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  **Incorrect or Missing Codes** – Using outdated or incorrect CPT and ICD-10 codes can lead to claim denials.
2.  **Lack of Medical Necessity** – Insurance payers may deny claims if the diagnosis does not support the necessity of the procedure.
3.  **Incomplete Documentation** – Missing patient information, referral details, or prior authorizations can cause claim rejections.
4.  **Credentialing Issues** – Delays in provider credentialing can result in claim holds and payment delays.
5.  **Timely Filing Errors** – Submitting claims past the payer’s deadline can lead to automatic denials.
6.  **Improper Modifier Usage** – Neurology services often require specific modifiers, and incorrect use can lead to claim denials.
