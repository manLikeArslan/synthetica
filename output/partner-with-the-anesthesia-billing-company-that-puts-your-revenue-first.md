FAQs:

**Q: How do anesthesia billing companies calculate anesthesia time?**

**A:** Anesthesia time starts when the provider begins patient care and ends when they are no longer responsible. Accurate tracking of this time ensures correct billing.

**Q: What are the most common anesthesia billing CPT codes?**

**A:** The most commonly used anesthesia CPT codes range from 00100 to 01999, which cover various anesthesia services related to surgical procedures. For a comprehensive guide on anesthesia billing codes and their accurate use, you can refer to the [American Society of Anesthesiologists (ASA)](https://www.asahq.org/).
