**Understanding Revenue Cycle Management**
------------------------------------------

### **What is RCM?**

[Revenue Cycle Management](https://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/revenue-management-services.aspx) is the financial backbone of every healthcare practice. It involves managing the complete billing cycle, from patient registration to final payment collection.

### **Key Challenges in RCM Faced by Healthcare Practices**

*   Delayed reimbursements due to claim denials
*   Inefficient tracking of patient payments
*   Lack of actionable data for revenue improvement

**Key Metrics in MBC’s Financial Dashboards**
---------------------------------------------

### **Accounts Receivable (AR) Analysis**

*   **AR Days:** Highlights the average time taken to collect payments.
*   **AR Aging Categories:** Breaks down overdue accounts for targeted follow-ups.

### **Denial Trends and Recovery Rates**

Track denial patterns, identify root causes, and measure recovery success rates.

### **Cash Flow and Collections Performance**

Evaluate overall cash flow to ensure steady revenue streams.

### **Patient Payment Metrics**

Understand patient payment behaviors to refine billing strategies and improve satisfaction.
