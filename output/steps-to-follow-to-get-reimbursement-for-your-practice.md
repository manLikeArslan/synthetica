FAQs

### 1\. How can I ensure timely reimbursements for my practice?

Verify patient insurance, submit clean claims, and follow up on denials promptly.

### 2\. What are the most common reasons for claim denials?

Incorrect coding, missing documentation, and eligibility issues often lead to denials.

### 3\. How does prior authorization affect reimbursements?

Without prior approval for certain procedures, insurance may reject the claim, delaying payment.

### 4\. Why is proper medical documentation important for billing?

Detailed documentation supports medical necessity and helps prevent claim denials.
