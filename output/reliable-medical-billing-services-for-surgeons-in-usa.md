### Ability to Handle Eligibility Verification and Pre-Authorizations

Eligibility verification helps determine that the patient is eligible to receive the treatment. Conversely, pre-authorization is a process of obtaining approvals for complex procedures from payers to ensure patient coverage. Accurate management of eligibility verification and pre-auth approvals prevents claim denials.

Complex surgeries have higher costs, thereby increasing the need for payer pre-treatment approvals. Obtaining payer approvals gives surgeons a sense of security that their services won’t be written off and that they’ll receive payment. Thus, hiring medical billing services for surgeons enable you to maximize reimbursements and reduce billing errors.

### Expertise in HCPCS, CPT, and ICD-10 Coding Specific to Surgery

The American Medical Association (AMA) and the Centers for Medicaid & Medicare (CMS) have specific codes to bill payers. All the medical procedures have different codes, and AAPC-certified coders help surgeons convert the rendered services into designated codes. Accurate coding is critical for surgeons to receive complete and timely payments for inpatient and outpatient surgery billing.

### Knowledge of Payer-Specific Guidelines and Compliance

There are specific healthcare rules and regulations to ensure compliance during the claim submission process. Medical billing services for surgeons need to follow the latest HIPAA and payers’ guidelines. Likewise, billing compliance is a basic necessity to streamline billing and financial operations. Claim denials can drain your overall revenue. Therefore, it is necessary to appeal the claim with accuracy to receive denied payments.
