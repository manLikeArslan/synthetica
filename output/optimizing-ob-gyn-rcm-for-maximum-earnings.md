**Understanding OB-GYN RCM:**
-----------------------------

OB-GYN RCM encompasses all administrative and clinical functions that contribute to capturing, managing, and collecting patient service revenue. Effective RCM in an OB/GYN practice ensures that every step in the patient care journey––from initial appointment scheduling to final payment––is optimized for financial performance. 

### **Key Elements of OB-GYN RCM:**

1.  **Patient Registration and Scheduling**: Accurate and efficient patient registration and scheduling are the first steps to prevent claim denials.
2.  **Insurance Verification**: Verifying patient insurance details before services are rendered can prevent billing issues.
3.  **Medical Coding**: Correct CPT and ICD-10 codes are essential for accurate billing.
