What is Medical Billing?
------------------------

It is the process of converting medical services into codes to submit claims to payers, along with accurate documentation. Accurate documentation ensures claims are clean and compliant for providers to receive timely and rightful reimbursements. As a provider, you don’t have to do it yourself when the top medical billing companies in USA are there to help you achieve financial stability.
