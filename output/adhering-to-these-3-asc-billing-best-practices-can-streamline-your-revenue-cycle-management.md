### To upgrade your revenue cycle and achieve long term financial success, your center needs to follow some best practices:

#### Precision in Coding

An ASC’s billing staff needs to comprehend coding complexities that are particular to the business. In the event that a claim is coded inaccurately, it can result in postponed payment, the requirement for an appeal, or sending a client into collections. In turn, this adversely impacts both AR days and income cycle.

To better depict a code without changing its definition, many ASCs need to bill codes with modifiers. In the event that this is not done accurately, it may bring about a drop in revenue, as well as result in a fine if the incorrect coding conflicts with Medicare rules.
