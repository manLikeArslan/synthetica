DME is any equipment that provides therapeutic benefits to a member because of certain medical conditions and/or illnesses that can withstand repeated use, is primarily and customarily used to serve a medical purpose, and is appropriate for use in the home. Rules revolving [DME billing](https://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/speciality/durable-medical-equipment-medical-billing-services.html) given the two major categories-purchase and renting DME, accompanied by regional differences in Medicare coverage, amongst many more issues; pose various challenges for DME with respect to reimbursement and profitability. Depending on recommendations from orthopedics and requiring claims to be submitted in a specific manner, DME entails a unique pattern of billing and coding.
