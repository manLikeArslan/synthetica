Why Denials Happen
------------------

Denial management and follow-up billing services in the US must first identify why claims get rejected. The most common reasons include:

*   Lack of medical necessity
    
*   Non-covered services or outdated codes
    
*   Untimely filing
    
*   Eligibility issues
    

Understanding these patterns is key to reducing future denials and strengthening your [**Revenue Cycle Management**](https://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/revenue-management-services.aspx).
