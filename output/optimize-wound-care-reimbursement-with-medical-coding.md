**Common CPT codes for Wound Care:**
------------------------------------

Wound care encompasses a wide range of procedures, each assigned a specific code for billing purposes. Procedures such as debridement, dressing changes, and wound assessments have unique codes that must be accurately documented.

### **Debridement Services:**

*   **CPT 11042 – 11047:** Codes within this range cover the debridement of skin and subcutaneous tissue. The specific code used depends on the size and complexity of the wound.

### **Skin Substitutes and Grafts:**

*   **CPT 15002 – 15431:** This range includes codes for the application of skin substitute grafts, with variations for different procedures and sizes of the treated area.

### **Wound Repair:**

*   **CPT 12001 – 13160:** These codes encompass wound repair procedures, including closures and repairs of various complexities.

### **Wound Evaluation and Management:**

*   **CPT 99201 – 99499:** These codes cover evaluation and management services for wounds, including office or other outpatient visits.

### **Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy:**

*   **CPT 99183:** This code is used for reporting hyperbaric oxygen therapy, which can be applicable in certain wound care scenarios.
