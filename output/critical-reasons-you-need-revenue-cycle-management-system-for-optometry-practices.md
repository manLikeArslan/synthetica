The RCM Workflow
----------------

Communicating with health insurance providers is another critical aspect of revenue management. When a patient books an appointment, the healthcare center typically checks the patient’s described insurance coverage before the visit.

After an insured patient gets diagnosed, he supplies the applicable copay. A healthcare provider or coder classifies the nature of the treatment as per the ICD-9 or ICD-10. The hospital submits the care summary with ICD and Current Procedural Technology codes to the patient’s insurance firm to see the procedure of treatment covered by insurance.
