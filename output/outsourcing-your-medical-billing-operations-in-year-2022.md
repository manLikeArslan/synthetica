Unique Medical Billing Operations in the Year 2022
--------------------------------------------------

With a considerable number of claims being denied, most medical practices have struggled during the COVID-19 pandemic. Recently, Apollo Healthcare has published its finding on how physicians are coping with the unique challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic.

According to the ‘2022 Apollo Healthcare Predictions Report’, US doctors are more than twice as likely to feel burned out compared to their European counterparts. The same report also highlighted staffing shortages as the main concern in practice management. Almost 45 percent of US physicians acknowledged staffing shortage as the main area of concern.

This stress of burnout and a lack of support staff has led to one in three US doctors considering leaving the profession in the past year, and this year’s number (34 percent) is nearly double what it was in 2018. With a primary focus on patient care, most providers would like to stay away from the administrative work of medical billing.
