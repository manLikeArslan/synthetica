**Why Do OB-GYN Practices Over-Code?**

There are two ways that providers over-code either through up-coding of unbundling of services.

Over coding occurs when a provider code for a more serious, and more expensive, procedure than was actually performed. Unbundling is the breaking out of a group of procedures that are considered all-inclusive into separate charges. If your practice is knowingly up-coding or unbundling stop it right away; you are breaking the law.
