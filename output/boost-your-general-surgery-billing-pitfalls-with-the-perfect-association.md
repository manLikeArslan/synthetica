### **What are the common pitfalls in general surgery billing and coding?**

Common pitfalls include errors in global surgical package billing, incorrect use of modifiers for surgeon roles and same-day procedures, and improper documentation of unlisted procedures. These mistakes can lead to claim denials, compliance issues, and reduced reimbursements.

### **What role does documentation play in general surgery billing?**

Documentation is critical in capturing the full scope of services provided before, during, and after a procedure. Accurate documentation ensures compliance with CPT and HCPCS guidelines, which is essential for maximizing reimbursements and avoiding audit risks.

### **Why is it important to use the correct modifiers in general surgery billing?**

Proper use of modifiers clarifies surgeon roles, same-day procedures, and specific circumstances that may affect billing. Incorrect modifiers can lead to claim rejections or lower reimbursements, impacting your practice’s financial health.
