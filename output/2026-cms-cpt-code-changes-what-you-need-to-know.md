# 2026 CMS CPT Code Changes: What You Need to Know

*Original URL: https://docsmedicalbilling.com/2026-cms-cpt-code-changes-what-you-need-to-know/*

---

The 2026 CPT code set introduces 288 new codes, 84 deletions, and 46 revisions, totaling 418 changes effective January 1, 2026. These updates from the American Medical Association (AMA) and Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) reflect advances in digital health, AI, and procedural care, directly impacting medical billing accuracy and reimbursement. Healthcare providers must update systems and workflows to avoid denials and ensure compliance

New codes emphasize shorter RPM durations and AI augmentation. Five codes cover 2-15 days of monitoring within a 30-day period, and two enable RPM treatment management after just 10 minutes monthly (previously 20).

CMS’s CY 2026 PFS final rule adjusts conversion factors and coverage, with some specialties and services seeing payment increases and others decreases—details vary by code family and must be checked in the final rule tables rather than assuming a uniform percentage change (such as +2.50%).  
Revenue opportunities arise from granular RPM billing and outpatient vascular codes, but expect Q1 2026 denial spikes. Dental/medical billers should crosswalk top-used codes and test claims.
