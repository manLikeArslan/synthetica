Understanding Provider Enrollment and Credentialing
---------------------------------------------------

Provider enrollment involves the process of registering healthcare providers with various insurance plans, government agencies, and other entities to become authorized to deliver medical services. It requires meticulous attention to detail and compliance with specific regulations and requirements.

Credentialing, on the other hand, is the evaluation and verification of a provider’s qualifications, experience, and competence to ensure they meet the standards set by regulatory bodies and insurance plans.
