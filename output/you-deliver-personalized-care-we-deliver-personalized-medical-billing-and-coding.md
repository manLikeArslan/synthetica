**FAQs**

**1\. What is Medical Billing and Coding?**

**Answer**: Medical billing and coding is the process of translating healthcare services into standardized codes and submitting them to insurance companies for reimbursement. Medical billers handle the submission and follow-up of claims, while medical coders assign specific codes to each diagnosis and procedure.

**2\. How Long Does it Take to Process Medical Billing Claims?**

**Answer**: The time to process a medical billing claim varies depending on the complexity of the services provided and the responsiveness of the payer. On average, claims can take anywhere from a few days to several weeks. At MBC, we streamline the process to ensure faster submissions and reduce delays.

**3\. How Do Medical Billing Companies Help Reduce Denials?**

**Answer**: Medical billing companies like MBC help reduce denials by ensuring accurate coding, timely submission of claims, and proactive denial management strategies. We carefully review claims for potential errors and resubmit denied claims quickly to avoid revenue loss.

**4\. What Are the Benefits of Outsourcing Medical Billing and Coding?**

**Answer**: Outsourcing medical billing and coding can save time, reduce administrative burdens, and increase revenue for healthcare providers. Professionals like MBC handle the entire billing cycle, freeing up your team to focus on patient care while ensuring claims are processed efficiently.

**5\. How Can I Improve Cash Flow with Medical Billing?**

**Answer**: Improving cash flow through medical billing involves submitting clean claims, reducing denials, following up on unpaid claims, and ensuring compliance with payer guidelines. MBC provides customized solutions to optimize your billing cycle, ensuring faster payments and consistent cash flow.
