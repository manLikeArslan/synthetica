# Popularity of ASC's in Billing Charges & payout Volumes

*Original URL: https://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/blog/popularity-of-ascs-in-billing-charges-payout-volumes/*

---

Ambulatory Surgical Centers are the latest trend and have become increasingly convenient for the patients. This has taken the [billing charges for ASC’s](https://www.medicalbillersandcoders.com/0-0-ambulatorysurgicalcenters-medical-billing.html) on a higher side, owing to the number of procedures for various specialties catered to. Based on a recent Industry overview, it has been reported that, Ophthalmology makes up the largest percentage of procedures performed in ambulatory surgery centers.

Ophthalmology, GI & Pain management are all thought of as high volume but low reimbursements and remain a stalwart for ASCs for several reasons. _Orthopedics_ is affected more by commercial reimbursement, and in ASCs yields a sizable volume and the highest average reimbursement.
